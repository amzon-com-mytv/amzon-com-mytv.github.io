How do I activate my Amazon account on my TV?


As you purchase Amazon subscription, link devices to stream Amazon. Simply,

  1. Open Amazon App on TV or other streaming devices & login your account. A code appears now.
  2. Then navigate to amazon official website, type Amazon.com/mytv on web browser.
  3. Enter the Code on the activation page as shown on your device.
  4. Add your device by simply clicking on the 'Submit' button.
  5. Sign in Amazon account using Login Email & Password. Click 'Continue' button.
  6. After completing activation steps you can stream Amazon content.
